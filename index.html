HOW IT WORKS

  1. The worker visits your blogspot page (e.g. siiirjune1.blogspot.com/p/mci.html)
  2. It extracts the JWPlayer stream URL (m3u8/mpd) from the page HTML
  3. All stream segments are proxied through Cloudflare's edge network
  4. Your browser only connects to the CF worker — not the blogspot/source directly
  5. This bypasses UAE geo-blocks since CF edge servers are not restricted
